History

We proudly uphold the values VKellogg's instilled more than 100 years ago — and now we’re doing it in 180 countries across the world including India and South Asia. We provide you and your family better breakfasts that lead to better days, and we flake corn the same way V.K. Kellogg did back in 1898. It just tastes better that way!


VKellogg's founded our company through his belief in nutrition and dedication to well-being. Motivated by a passion for people, quality, and innovation, he created the first-ever breakfast cereal and then shaped an entire industry. Kellogg soon became a household name; his signature, a trusted mark. Today, his legacy continues to inspire us. Working together, we create moments of delight for people around the world with our well-loved brands.

Vision

To be the global leader in breakfast foods and snacks, renowned for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to create delightful and nutritious products that enrich lives and build lasting relationships with customers worldwide.


Mission

Our mission at VKellogg's is to provide nutritious and delicious food options that bring smiles to our customers' faces. We are committed to sustainability, innovation, and making a positive impact on the communities we serve.
